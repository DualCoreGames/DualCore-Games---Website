VR & XR Simulation Services

Immersive, high-fidelity spatial computing solutions. We engineer enterprise-grade VR and XR simulations that deliver measurable training outcomes and interactive marketing experiences.

Our VR simulation services are built on the same hardcore performance principles that power our commercial games.

Virtual reality development requires an uncompromising focus on framerate and stereoscopic rendering stability.

A dropped frame doesn't just look bad; it induces physical motion sickness. By applying rigorous AAA optimization techniques, we deliver VR simulation services that ensure comfort and immersion. See our VR/XR Portfolio for practical examples of our enterprise integrations.

Overview & Philosophy

The leap from 2D screens to full spatial immersion introduces radical technical challenges. Standard interaction paradigms fail, and conventional rendering metrics simply do not apply when drawing two independent 4K viewports at 90Hz. At DualCore Games, our VR simulation services treat these constraints as foundational design rules rather than obstacles.

We collaborate intimately with enterprise clients, educational institutions, and healthcare providers to translate complex real-world operations into intuitive virtual operations. We ensure that physical interactions feel grounded and that critical data is visualized logically within the 3D space, heavily mitigating the learning curve for non-gamers.

Key Advantages

  • Ergonomic Interaction: Custom physics-based hand interactions that feel weighty and realistic, rather than floaty.
  • Uncompromising Framerates: Strict 72/90/120 FPS targets maintained through aggressive batching and custom shaders.
  • Hardware Portability: Graceful graphical scaling from tethered PC-VR down to standalone mobile headsets.
  • Analytics Integration: Invisible telemetry systems that track gaze, interaction times, and specific failure states for training assessment.

Deliverables

Enterprise Training Simulators

High-stakes industrial, medical, and aviation training environments where failure is expensive or dangerous. We recreate complex machinery controls and physics systems, providing detailed onboarding programs that accelerate skill acquisition safely. Our VR simulation services excel at transforming 500-page manuals into visceral, hands-on learning experiences.

Architectural Visualization (ArchViz)

Photorealistic virtual walkthroughs for unbuilt real estate, engineering mockups, and retail layout planning. We utilize Unreal Engine 5's Nanite and Lumen technologies, baked down specifically for XR constraints, delivering stunning visual fidelity that accurately represents lighting, material finish, and spatial scale before physical construction begins.

Interactive Brand Experiences

Engaging promotional activations for trade shows and marketing campaigns. Whether it's a gamified product demonstration or a fully interactive metaverse activation, we engineer reliable applications that can run unattended for 12 hours a day without crashing or memory-leaking.

Mixed & Augmented Reality (MR/AR)

Blending digital overlays seamlessly with the user's physical environment. Utilizing the passthrough capabilities of the latest headsets (Meta Quest 3, Apple Vision Pro), our XR and VR simulation services provide context-aware data visualization, spatial mapping, and persistent holographic interfaces anchored directly to real-world objects.

Our Process

Spatial application development demands a unique prototyping velocity. We iterate constantly inside the headset, ensuring interactions never exist merely as theoretical design documents.

01

Spatial Prototyping

Greyboxing the environment to test scale, reachability, and nausea-free locomotion systems immediately. We validate UI placement and core physical interactions before any high-poly art is committed.

02

Asset Optimization & Logic

Creating dedicated VR-ready assets utilizing texture atlases and heavy LOD hierarchies. We wire the underlying simulation logic and integrate external telemetry endpoints into the specific hardware platform's SDK.

03

Performance Profiling

Relentless frame timing analysis using specific GPU profilers (like Snapdragon Profiler for mobile). We ensure the minimum frame rate never dips beneath the hardware refresh rate to guarantee total user comfort.
