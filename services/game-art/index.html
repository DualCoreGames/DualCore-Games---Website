Game Art Services

Visual excellence meets technical constraint. Outsource and co-develop production-ready assets and environments that preserve your creative vision while maximizing real-time performance.

Our game art services are designed for scalability, ensuring your art direction translates seamlessly to the final build.

From specialized hard-surface modeling and stylized character rigs to sprawling AAA-fidelity environments, we provide comprehensive game art services tailored to fit the precise technical requirements of modern game systems.

We don't just create beautiful 3D geometry; we engineer optimized digital assets that render flawlessly on target hardware. Check out our Game Art Portfolio to see our capabilities in action.

Overview & Philosophy

At DualCore Games, our approach to game art is rooted in a deep understanding of the engine pipeline. Beautiful concept art means nothing if it crushes your frame rate. That is why our technical artists and 3D modelers work in tandem.

By defining clear polygon budgets, texture memory ceilings, and shader complexity limits upfront, integrating our game art services into your production pipeline is seamless. We speak the language of engineering, delivering source files and prefabs that drop right into your scene structure without causing merge conflicts or rendering errors.

Key Advantages

  • Pipeline Integration: Assets delivered as engine-ready prefabs, complete with colliders and LODs.
  • Technical Validation: Continuous profiling and bounds checking against target hardware specifications.
  • Scalable Production: Ramping up from small prop batches to entirely outsourced environment construction.
  • Art Direction Adherence: Strict compliance with established style guides and concept reference materials.

Deliverables

3D Assets & Props

High-to-low poly workflows, clean quad topology, and Physically Based Rendering (PBR) texturing. We ensure every asset is performance-optimized, UV-mapped efficiently to minimize draw calls, and integration-ready for Unity or Unreal Engine. Whether it's realistic weaponry or stylized fantasy elements, our game art services cover the full spectrum of prop creation.

Technical Art & Shaders

Custom shader development, particle effects, and rigging. We solve the complex visual challenges that bridge art and engineering. By leveraging tools like Shader Graph, HLSL, and node-based material editors, we create bespoke visual effects that run perfectly on both mobile devices and high-end PCs.

Environment Design

Comprehensive world-building, lighting, and level dressing. We create immersive spaces that guide player experience and storytelling. Our environment teams specialize in modular kit creation, allowing for rapid level design iteration without blowing past your geometry budgets or texture streaming limits.

VFX & Particles

Dynamic visual effects designed for impact without sacrificing frame rates. Expert use of Unity VFX Graph, Shuriken, and Unreal Niagara. We author explosive combat effects, subtle ambient particles, and reactive environmental details that bring virtual worlds to life.

Our Process

A predictable, transparent pipeline is essential when outsourcing game art services. We follow a strict methodology to ensure that what we deliver matches your expectations perfectly the first time.

01

Styleguide & Blocking

Establishing visual benchmarks and technical constraints to ensure consistency across the project. We create blockouts and greybox models for game design approval before committing to high-res sculpting.

02

Production & Texturing

Efficient asset creation with regular feedback loops, maintaining clean quad topology. We bake high-poly details onto optimized low-poly meshes and author PBR material sets customized for your engine's lighting model.

03

Integration & QA

Direct engine deployment and testing to ensure assets look and perform as intended. We check for bounding box issues, collision overlap, LOD switching distances, and material memory overhead before final delivery.
