01 / The Arcade Revival

Nostalgia Engineered.

Drawing direct inspiration from timeless classics like Contra, Super Mario, and Prince of Persia, Crazy Planet is designed to recreate the visceral thrill of 90s arcade gaming. The game spans 7 progressively difficult levels, each introducing new gameplay elements to continuously test the player's reflexes.

We engineered the movement physics to feel immediate and predictable, ensuring that every missed jump or trap collision feels like a learning opportunity rather than a technical flaw.

02 / Precision & Combat

Adapt to Survive.

The world is filled with hostile creatures, wild animals, and complex mechanical traps. Rather than relying on simple hitpoint attrition, we built encounters where players must analyze specific enemy behaviors and strike at the perfect moment.

The progression system is strictly built on the classic design philosophy: Teach → Test → Reward. New mechanics are introduced safely before being combined into punishing challenge sequences.
