PC / Horror Prototype

Never Open The Door

An atmospheric horror experience built to explore systemic AI behavior and dynamic tension loops.

Never Open The Door Concept Visual

Overview

Never Open The Door is a technical prototype designed to test advanced AI perception systems and environmental storytelling. The core loop centers around sound-based detection and psychological horror elements, requiring a robust state-machine architecture for the antagonistic entities.

Technical Challenges

The primary challenge was implementing an AI system that felt "fair" yet unpredictable. This involved a multi-layered perception system (visual, audio, and footprint detection) that interacts with a dynamic environment, allowing players to use the surrounding space to hide or distract threats.

Key Systems

Acoustic Propagation

Real-time sound simulation where audio cues bounce off surfaces and lose intensity over distance, affecting AI detection.

Behavior Trees

Clean, modular AI behaviors that switch between roaming, searching, and chasing states based on environmental stimuli.
