Reclairos

A Dark Strategy Experience Where Systems Shape the Battle.

Reclairos is a system-driven strategy game built around tactical combat encounters, evolving enemy behaviors, and performance-first design.

Genre Dark Strategy Action RPG
Engine Unity
Platforms PC / Mobile
Focus Tactical combat encounters

The World of Reclairos

Reclairos takes place in a fractured realm where ancient powers and forgotten kingdoms collide.

Across ruined landscapes and corrupted battlefields, powerful factions struggle to control the remnants of a world shaped by war and lost civilizations.

The balance of power has collapsed, leaving scattered strongholds and hostile forces competing for dominance.

In this unstable world, every battle matters.

Players step into a conflict where strategy, timing, and battlefield awareness determine survival.

Your Role in the Conflict

In Reclairos, players command a roster of unique characters, each built around distinct combat roles.

Rather than relying on raw power alone, victory depends on understanding how different units interact during combat encounters.

Players must deploy characters strategically, respond to evolving enemy formations, and maintain control of the battlefield as encounters grow increasingly complex.

Success comes from reading the battlefield and adapting quickly to changing threats.

Core Gameplay Experience

Reclairos delivers tactical combat encounters where strategy emerges from the interaction between characters, enemy archetypes, and encounter design.

Players must:

  • Deploy units with specific combat roles
  • Counter enemy archetypes
  • Maintain battlefield positioning
  • Adapt strategies as encounters evolve

Each stage introduces new enemy combinations and tactical situations, ensuring that the gameplay remains engaging while staying readable and responsive.

Enemy Forces

The enemies of Reclairos are designed around distinct archetypes that challenge the player in different ways.

Some enemies pressure the frontline with relentless attacks, while others disrupt positioning or attack from range.

As players progress, encounters introduce new enemy combinations that require different tactical responses.

By building encounters around enemy behavior rather than simple difficulty scaling, Reclairos creates combat scenarios that feel dynamic and strategic.

Gameplay Pillars

Strategic Combat

Battles reward planning and positioning instead of relying only on reaction speed.

Distinct Hero Roles

Heroes serve different combat functions such as frontline defense, ranged damage, and tactical disruption.

Enemy Archetypes

Enemies introduce unique behaviors and patterns that challenge player strategy.

System-Driven Progression

Encounters evolve as players progress, introducing new enemy combinations and tactical situations.

World Exploration

The world of Reclairos features exploration-based progression, taking players through dense villages, ancient ruins, and high-tension encounter zones across a fractured landscape.
