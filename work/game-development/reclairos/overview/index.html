Your Commanders

Assemble Your Elite Squad: Tactical RPG Class Synergy

Five distinct combat roles. Infinite tactical combinations. Encounters are designed so no single unit type dominates — strategy demands synergy.

🛡️
Vanguard

Frontline Tank

High HP. Absorbs damage and slows the enemy advance. Buys critical time for ranged units to operate.

🏹
Ranger

Ranged DPS

Consistent damage from a safe distance. Vulnerable to fast disruptors that bypass the frontline.

✨
Warden

Support / Healer

Sustains the frontline with buffs and heals. Critical in grueling multi-wave encounters.

💥
Breaker

Burst / AoE Damage

Devastating burst output against grouped enemies. Long cooldown. Obliterates dense wave clusters.

⚡
Sentinel

Crowd Control

Applies slows, stuns, and traps. Counters fast disruptors. Low DPS, maximum utility.

Know Your Enemy

Combat Intel: Data-Driven Enemy Archetypes

Wave-based enemy archetypes designed with hard counters. Encounter them unprepared, and your escort won't survive.

Combat Intel — Archetype 01

Aggressive Melee

Rushes the escort directly. High pressure, low HP. Counter: Frontline Vanguards + Sentinel slows.

Combat Intel — Archetype 02

Heavy Defender

Blocks the lane. Absorbs damage. Counter: Breaker burst damage + flanking Ranger pressure.

Combat Intel — Archetype 03

Fast Disruptor

Bypasses the frontline. Targets ranged and support units at the rear. Counter: Sentinel traps + Vanguard reposition.

World Exploration: Episodic Narrative Chapters

Reclairos features exploration-based chapter progression, taking your squad through dense villages, ancient ruins, and high-tension encounter zones across a fractured, episodic world.

Early Chapters

Late Chapters

Three+ archetypes. Elite enemies. Aggressive escort pathing.
