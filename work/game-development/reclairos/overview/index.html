Your Commanders

Assemble Your Elite Squad

Five distinct combat roles. Infinite tactical combinations. Encounters are designed so no single unit type dominates — strategy demands synergy.

🛡️ Vanguard

Frontline Tank

High HP. Absorbs damage and slows the enemy advance. Buys critical time for ranged units.

🏹 Ranger

Ranged DPS

Consistent damage from a safe distance. Vulnerable to fast disruptors that bypass the frontline.

✨ Warden

Support / Healer

Sustains the frontline with buffs and heals. Critical in grueling multi-wave encounters.

💥 Breaker

Burst / AoE Damage

Devastating burst output against grouped enemies. Long cooldown. Use to obliterate dense clusters.

⚡ Sentinel

Crowd Control

Applies slows, stuns, and traps. Counters fast disruptor archetypes. Low DPS, high utility.
