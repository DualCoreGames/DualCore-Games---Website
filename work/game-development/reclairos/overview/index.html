Project Overview

Reclairos is a system-driven strategy game built around tactical combat encounters, evolving enemy behaviors, and performance-first design.

Rather than relying on scripted sequences, Reclairos utilizes a modular architecture where exploration, narrative triggers, and environmental systems operate in a tightly connected loop.

Core Experience

Reclairos delivers tactical combat encounters where strategy emerges from the interaction between characters, enemy archetypes, and encounter design.

Tactical Deployment

Positioning and role-based synergy.

Turn-based depth and AI behaviors.
