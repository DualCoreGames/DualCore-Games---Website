Technical Summary

Our medical simulations are built on a performance-first architecture that prioritizes haptic feedback and physics accuracy. The core challenge was synchronizing complex soft-body deformations with sub-millimeter input precision on standalone hardware.

By implementing a custom GPGPU-based physics solver, we achieved real-time organic tissue interaction without compromising the 90Hz refresh rate required for surgical VR immersion.
