01 / Redefining the Level

The Room as a Canvas.

Room Raider represents the pinnacle of Spatial Computing at DualCore. We moved beyond virtual skyboxes to treat the player's physical environment as the primary gameplay arena.

By leveraging the latest Mixed Reality SDKs, we developed a proprietary Occlusion Masking System that allows for sub-centimeter accuracy when blending virtual characters with physical furniture.

02 / Spatial Persistence

Digital Permanence

One of the biggest challenges in MR is ensuring that virtual objects feel like they have 'weight' in the real world. Our Persistent Anchor Registry ensures that every virtual collectible or hazard placed in your room is saved in physical space.

This allows for truly multi-session experiences where your room evolves over time as you interact with the digital layer of Room Raider.
