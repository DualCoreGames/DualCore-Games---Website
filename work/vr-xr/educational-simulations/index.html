Technical Summary

Our educational simulations leverage spatial learning to increase engagement and retention. The technical focus is on maintaining high-fidelity 3D environments on mobile VR chipsets while logging complex interaction data for pedagogical analysis.

By utilizing aggressive geometry baking and single-pass instanced rendering, we ensure constant 90Hz stability, critical for reducing cognitive load and preventing simulation sickness during learning sessions.
