01 / Digital Heritage

Cinema Reimagined.

ShemarooVerse is a flagship Metaversal Experience built for one of the largest media brands in the world. We translated decades of cinematic history into a production-ready VR platform.

By utilizing our Cinematic Shader Pipeline, we achieved a "theatrical" visual quality on mobile XR chipsets, allowing fans to experience brand heritage with unparalleled clarity and depth.

02 / Synchronized Social

Shared Narratives

At the heart of ShemarooVerse is its Social Sync Engine. We developed a low-latency network layer that ensures every user in a virtual cinema sees the same frame of content simultaneously, regardless of their global location.

This technical foundation enables high-impact brand events, movie premieres, and fan meetups within a persistent virtual space.
