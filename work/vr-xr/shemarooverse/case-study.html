Technical Deep Dive

Engineering Shemarooverse: Next-Gen Content Delivery

Constructing a high-end, immersive content consumption and social platform in VR for a major entertainment network.

Shemarooverse Visual Demo

Project Overview

Shemarooverse represents a strategic shift in media consumption, moving a colossal, legacy media library into an interactive, spatial environment. We partnered with Shemaroo Entertainment to build a branded virtual world where users can consume licensed content, interact with proprietary IP, and engage in shared social viewing experiences.

Technical Challenges

Delivering high-definition, DRM-protected video inside a 3D environment on mobile-class VR hardware (Meta Quest) introduces severe decoding bottlenecks. Traditional 2D UI paradigms also fail in spatial mediums, requiring a complete redesign of the content discovery UX.

Architecture & Systems

We developed a custom media pipeline integrated directly into Unity's rendering loop, utilizing hardware-accelerated decoding. The backend interfaces with Shemaroo's existing CMS via a bespoke GraphQL layer, ensuring content updates in the VR world reflect their web platforms in real-time.

Performance Engineering

To maintain 72-90hz while decoding 4K video streams, we offloaded UI rendering to independent render targets and utilized asynchronous stereoscopic timewarp. The environmental assets were optimized using aggressive texture atlasing and pre-computed localized light probes to keep graphic overhead minimal.

Platforms

Meta Quest 2, Meta Quest Pro, JioDive AR.
