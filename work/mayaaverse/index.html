Mayaaverse

UNREAL ENGINE 5 VR / XR SOCIAL PLATFORM INFRASTRUCTURE

A persistent virtual reality world focused on high-concurrency social interactions.

Mayaaverse World Showcase

Overview

Mayaaverse is a large-scale social VR platform featuring persistent environments, interactive media systems, and advanced avatars. Designed for thousands of concurrent users, it provides a foundation for virtual events, digital real estate, and immersive social experiences.

Platform Features

Persistent World Architecture

A modular environment system that ensures user-made changes and world states are saved across sessions.

Advanced Avatar Customization

High-fidelity characters with expressive animations and modular component systems for unique digital identities.

Immersive Social Systems

Real-time spatial audio and interactive props that facilitate natural human interaction in a virtual space.

Cross-Platform Connectivity

Seamless access across Meta Quest headsets, PC VR, and mobile companion applications.

Systems Implemented

  • Spatial Audio Replication
  • Real-time Real Estate Synchronization
  • Modular Temple Architecture
  • Quest-to-PC Optimization Pipeline
  • AI-Driven Environmental Interactions
  • Distributed Networking Layer
  • Persistent State Management
  • High-Concurrency Socket Server

Platforms

Meta Quest 2/3, PC VR, Mobile (iOS/Android)

Project Metadata

  • Engine: Unreal Engine
  • Platform: VR / XR / Mobile
  • Category: Social VR Platform
  • Role: Technical Architecture
  • Focus: Infrastructure Engineering

What This Project Demonstrates

Mayaaverse demonstrates our expertise in building high-fidelity VR simulations at scale. It showcases our ability to handle complex multi-platform synchronization and high-performance rendering for stand-alone XR devices, while maintaining a persistent and scalable backend infrastructure.
