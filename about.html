About DualCore

DualCore is a performance-driven game development studio focused on building scalable interactive systems.

We specialize in PC, mobile, and immersive platforms, combining structured engineering with modular production pipelines.

Our intentional design delivers production-ready experiences built for real-world deployment.

We partner with studios, startups, and enterprises to architect systems that are commercially viable and technically resilient.

Production Experience

Our experience spans commercial games, multiplayer systems, immersive simulations, and cross-platform deployments. We prioritize stability, scalability, and measurable performance across every build.

  • 10+ interactive titles across major platforms
  • Advanced multiplayer & SDK integrations (Photon, Photon Voice)
  • Structured build pipelines and deployment workflows
  • Multi-platform deployments: PC, Mobile, VR (Meta Quest), immersive systems
  • Performance-focused architecture for consistent hardware execution

Engineering Meets Creative Discipline

At DualCore, creativity and engineering are developed in parallel.

We believe interactive systems succeed when technical architecture and artistic direction evolve together from day one.

This reduces technical debt, improves production velocity, and ensures stable long-term scalability.

Our work is defined by modular architecture, performance benchmarking, and transparent collaboration. Every system is built with commercial durability in mind.

How We Operate
